-Commands to checkout and commit
svn co https://codex.cs.bham.ac.uk/svn/team-project/D2 --username "username"
svn add "filename"
svn commit -m "log messages"

-Update
svn update https://codex.cs.bham.ac.uk/svn/team-project/D2 

-Delete
svn delete "filename"
svn commit -m "log messages" "filename"

-View content
svn list

-Diffrence
svn diff "filename"
